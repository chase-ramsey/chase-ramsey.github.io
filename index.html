About me

If I had to explain myself as simply as possible, I'd say that I'm a person who is interested in learning about everything. I have studied literature at the graduate level; written articles, news, and reviews about videogames from a critical perspective; spent countless hours diving deep in conversation with friends about music, movies, and TV shows; and now, worked my way through a six-month, full-stack web developer bootcamp. I couldn't be happier to find myself in a discipline with so much respect for creativity and opportunity to learn, and I'm excited to make my mark on the field.

My software development background is in front-end technologies—HTML, CSS, Javascript, and AngularJS, mainly—and in the Python/Django stack for server-side development. I like working on projects that present an interesting technical challenge, especially if the product helps enable new, meaningful conversations and experiences with users. I'm looking forward to joining a team where I can continue to grow as a developer and find new, lively interests to pursue.
